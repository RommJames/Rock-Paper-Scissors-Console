Battle of the Elements

Rock-Paper-Scissors Showdown

In a mystical realm where the elements collide, the legendary Guardian of Elements has summoned you to prove your worth! As a brave challenger, you must face off against the Guardian in a series of intense Rock-Paper-Scissors battles. Each victory brings you closer to unlocking the elemental powers of Rock, Paper, and Scissors. Will you harness the strength of the elements and emerge victorious?
